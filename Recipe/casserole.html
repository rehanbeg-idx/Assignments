Chicken Casserole

Description

Chicken Parmesan Casserole is a hearty, family-friendly dish that combines all the classic flavors of chicken parmesan in a warm and comforting bake. Tender chunks of chicken are layered with rich marinara sauce, melted mozzarella, and parmesan cheese, then topped with a crispy breadcrumb coating for extra crunch. Baked until golden and bubbly, this casserole is easy to prepare and perfect for busy weeknights or casual gatherings. It delivers the same delicious taste of traditional chicken parmesan but in a simple, one-pan style that makes serving and cleanup effortless.

Ingredients

  • Meat: Cooked and shredded chicken breasts or thighs form the base of this casserole.
  • Pasta: Penne, rotini, or ziti pasta works best to hold the sauce and cheese.
  • Marinara sauce: A jar of marinara or homemade tomato sauce adds rich flavor.
  • Cheese: Mozzarella and parmesan cheese give it that classic Chicken Parmesan taste, with ricotta or cream cheese for creaminess.
  • Breadcrumb: A mix of breadcrumbs, olive oil or butter, and extra parmesan creates a crispy, golden topping.
  • Seasonings: Italian seasoning, garlic powder, salt, and black pepper enhance the flavor.
  • Fresh herbs: Basil or parsley for garnish adds freshness and color.

Steps

  1. Gather all your ingredients.
  2. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a rolling boil over high heat.Cook rotini in boiling water until tender yet firm to the bite, about 8 minutes.
  3. Stir together cooked rotini, chicken, and mozzarella cheese in a large casserole dish.
  4. Pour marinara sauce over pasta mixture; sprinkle with bread crumbs. Cover the dish with aluminum foil.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted, about 35 minutes.
  6. Serve and enjoy.
